Process

Clear steps from first brief to final shipment.

You bring the product goal. We help turn it into a sourceable, inspectable, shippable plan.

  1. 01

    Product Brief

    We define the product, target pricing, packaging needs, quantities, timeline, and quality expectations.

  2. 02

    Vendor Search

    We compare supplier options, request quotes, review samples, and narrow the field to the best fit.

  3. 03

    Development

    Packaging, accessories, molds, artwork, samples, and revisions are coordinated before production begins.

  4. 04

    Production and QA/QC

    We track production progress and support quality checks before goods are approved for shipment.

  5. 05

    Freight to the States

    We help organize freight, documentation, and shipping coordination through delivery in the U.S.
